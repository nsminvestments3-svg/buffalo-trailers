About Buffalo Trailers

Buffalo Trailers is a trailer brand manufactured by NSM Investments3 Pty Ltd. We specialise in trailer manufacturing, trailer rentals, repairs, trailer parts, Data Dot fitment, and transport solutions. Our trailers are built with strength, practicality and reliability in mind for South African roads and business conditions.

Our Services

Trailer Manufacturing

Custom-built Buffalo Trailers designed for strength, durability and practical use.

Trailer Rentals

Short and medium-term trailer rentals for households, businesses and contractors.

Repairs & Maintenance

Professional trailer servicing, repairs, maintenance and roadworthy support.
